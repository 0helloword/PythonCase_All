*** Keywords ***
打开地址
    [Arguments]    ${url}
    关闭浏览器
    Open Browser    ${url}    chrome
    Maximize Browser Window

按钮点击
    [Arguments]    ${name}
    Wait Until Keyword Succeeds    60    2    click element    jquery=button:contains('${name}'):visible

菜单选择
    [Arguments]    ${name}
    Wait Until Keyword Succeeds    10    2    click element    jquery=ul>li>a:contains('${name}'):eq(0)

下拉框选择
    [Arguments]    ${name}    ${choose}
    Wait Until Keyword Succeeds    10    2    Select From List By Label    jquery=div:contains('${name}')+div>select    ${choose}

提交数据
    [Arguments]    ${name}
    Wait Until Keyword Succeeds    60    2    click element    jquery=div>a:contains('${name}')

选择时间
    [Arguments]    ${name}    ${time}
    Wait Until Keyword Succeeds    10    2    Execute Javascript    jQuery("div:contains('${name}')+div input").val('${time}')
    [Teardown]

单选点击
    [Arguments]    ${name}    ${value}
    Wait Until Keyword Succeeds    10    2    Wait Until Page Contains Element    jquery=div>span>input[name='${name}']
    Wait Until Keyword Succeeds    10    2    Select Radio Button    ${name}    ${value}

选择下拉框内容
    [Arguments]    ${name}    ${value}
    Wait Until Keyword Succeeds    10    2    Select From List By Label    jquery=select[name='${name}']    ${value}

关闭浏览器
    Close All Browsers

读取页面数据
    [Arguments]    ${name}
    ${value}    Get Value    jquery=div:contains('${name}')+div input
    Set Global Variable    ${value}
    [Return]    ${value}
